about us why choose us

what makes us special?

Welcome to Baratie, the world's first on-the-sea cafe! We are proud to offer our customers a unique dining experience, where they can enjoy a delicious cup of coffee while surrounded by the beauty of the ocean.

Our coffee beans are sourced locally and ethically, straight from the farmers. We believe that building strong relationships with our suppliers is essential to ensuring the quality of our coffee. We also work closely with our baristas, who are experienced professionals with a passion for coffee.

As you sip on your coffee, you can relax and take in the stunning views of the ocean. Baratie is located in a picturesque spot, where you can watch the waves crash against the shore and the seagulls soar overhead.

We invite you to come and experience the magic of Baratie for yourself. We are confident that you will love our delicious coffee, our friendly service, and our unique setting.
